We empower the global ambitious of leading innovative compannies

With strong ties in Latin America and global reach, Base is a innovation focused private investment partnership. We were formed prompted by the vision that there was an unmatched opportunity to leverage relationships and build a distinctive angle to venture capital investing globally.

Build + Connect + Add Value

We are growth investors and partners with leading VC firms, corporates and families to source and leverage innovative companies on the path to becoming global.

By combining our relationship driven mentality with our priviled network, Base built a distinctive angle to invest alongside top VC firms in growth stage US tech companies and help them go global.
Beyond capital, we are a strategic long term partner to both portfolio companies and VC funds, providing knowledge, perspective and being helpful.
We use our experience, LP community and extensive network in the region to help our portfolio companies with access, expertise, talent, policy and regulatory affairs and other key aspects involved in expanding into a new geography.
In late 2016 M Square Global partnered with Base. Such partnership provides us valuable intangible assets such as investing capabilities, infra-structure and the stamp of approval from one of the most renowned Brazilian investment franchises.

Base Labs is a powerful platform to disseminate knowledge and help bridge Brazilian corporates to disruptin technologies.

Base Labs converts our connections into knowledge. Through Base Labs we maintain a structured communication channel with CTO's, CIO's and CEO's of the largest conglomerates in Brazil and other countries in Latin America.
By being the eyes and ears of the Brazilian Corporates, we have the unique opportunity to understand their most critical areas while being able to share the knowledge gathered in our constant interactions with tech investors and companies.
Knowledge generated through Base labs becomes valuable to quantify opportunities in Brazil and to facilitate marketing, recruiting and business development for our portfolio companies as they think about expanding in the region.

Base Partners has the unique opportunity of having M Square Global team committed to the success of our firm.
