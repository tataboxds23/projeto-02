Build + Connect + Add Value

We are growth investors and partners with leading VC firms, corporates and families to source and leverage innovative companies on the path to becoming global.

By combining our relationship driven mentality with our priviled network, Base built a distinctive angle to invest alongside top VC firms in growth stage US tech companies and help them go global.
Beyond capital, we are a strategic long term partner to both portfolio companies and VC funds, providing knowledge, perspective and being helpful.
We use our experience, LP community and extensive network in the region to help our portfolio companies with access, expertise, talent, policy and regulatory affairs and other key aspects involved in expanding into a new geography.
In late 2016 M Square Global partnered with Base. Such partnership provides us valuable intangible assets such as investing capabilities, infra-structure and the stamp of approval from one of the most renowned Brazilian investment franchises.
